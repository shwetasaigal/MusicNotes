The golden ages is a period from the 1960's to the 1980's which gave us countless evergreen songs. The songs that we still hum to and have become our favourites. The versatality of talent offered by the playback singers and musc directors gave us songs for every occassion. From holi to weddings , romace to heart breakes, every emotion was beautifully captured in these songs.
