The music in early 80's saw a shift from raag based music to westernized songs. Retro beats and disco beats became popular in this decade. Songs like Ek Haseena Thi from Karz and Disco Deewane gained popularity and appealed to the masses.

The late 80's saw the re-emergence of romantic sogns with albums like Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak and Maine Pyaar Kiya and gave way to upcoming music directors
