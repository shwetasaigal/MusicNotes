The sixties saw major shifts in the music industry. Playback singing became an integral part of the movies. Lata Mangeshkar, Mukesh and Mohd. Rafi made their place with their distinct singing styles.

The musical compositions were raag based. Music Director Madan Mohan and S.D.Burman gave some immortal tunes.
